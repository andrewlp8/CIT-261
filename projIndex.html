About the Game

While growing up my family use to enjoy playing a game of spinning a globe with our eyes closed and point to a random location to see where we will serve on a mission. The object of the game is simple, to discover possibilities of where you may get called on a mission. I have decided to use this idea for a class project.

LDS Mission Fishin'

How to Play the Game

To play click the green play button, enter your information, click or touch the screen after the globe gets spinning, and find out where it takes you.

From there you may follow the link to learn more or start a new run to land yourself on another mission.

Enjoy!

LDS Mission Fishin'

While growing up my family use to enjoy playing a game of spinning a globe with our eyes closed and point to a random location to see where we will serve on a mission. I have decided to use this idea for a project, and will work on including the removal of any conditions outside of current actual mission possibilities, so, there will be no Atlantis missions... not yet at least.

The object of the game is simple, to discover possibilities of where yu may get called on a mission. To play click play, click on the spinning globe, and find out where it takes you. From there you may follow the link to learn more or start a new run to discover another mission.

Touch or click the spinning globe to see where you might serve!
